# No dropping the box
Privacy should not be a luxury. In an age where personal information can be scattered across multiple online platforms, understanding your digital footprint is more important than ever.

No Dropping the Box is an OSINT-based privacy analysis project designed to help users identify publicly exposed information, understand potential privacy risks, and take steps to better protect their online presence.

The project aims to provide a simple and accessible way to visualize digital exposure, analyze publicly available data, and generate actionable privacy recommendations.

Mission: Empower individuals to understand and protect their digital identity without compromising privacy, ethics, or security.
